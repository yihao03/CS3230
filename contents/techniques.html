2.1 Extensions

There are useful extensions to case 2 of Master Theorem handling more values of kkk. For example, consider the case where d=3d = 3d=3. If f(n)=n3log⁡nf(n) = n^3 \log nf(n)=n3logn, then we are in case 2 of Master Theorem with k=1k = 1k=1. However, Master Theorem as presented above does not handle the case where k<0k < 0k<0, e.g. when
